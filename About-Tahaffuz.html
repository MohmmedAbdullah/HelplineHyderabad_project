About Tahaffuz

Islam is a perfect and complete religion that has the status of eternal constitution and perpetual charter for the entire humanity until the Day of Resurrection and there is absolutely no possibility of any shortcoming; however, it is possible that the Ummat may face different situations with elapsing time and due to ignorance and negligence, disaffection of Book and Sunnat and apathy to cult (maslak) and elders may fall prey to them; but in this era of tribulations too, the path that Prophet(SAW) indicated through: That upon which I and my companions are; is a beacon and guidance principle for the entire Ummat until the last day.

In the midst of various groups and murky enticements in this present age, identification of truth and the righteous is becoming a tricky issue; everyone is associating oneself alone with truth and is engaged in pronouncing others infidels and misguided and in realising one’s own interests; likewise media is operating as a prominent weapon and basic ammunition to promote these invalid reflections and theories.

And it is also not concealed from anyone that through electronic and print media, the deception of the Ummat that is rampant today, the tribulations that are evident today and the extent of hostilities against Islam and Ahle-Sunnat-Wal-Jamat today have fewer and in fact rare similitude in the past. Now is the time to seek refuge in Al-Hafeez: Oh my body is covered all over in wounds; to which of these should I apply the lint?

In this perilous, if fact regrettable situation, wherein on account of successive invasions and constant attacks of falsehood, the Muslim nation is groaning, various factions and movements together have scattered the collective appeal of the Ummat and the unfinished efforts to distort the image of Islam at internal and external levels are on the rise.

In this context, a few scholars felt the urgent need to establish such organisation under the direct supervision of elders that being a manifestation of conventional good and contemporary benefit is equipped with the prominent weapon of media to perform the task of protecting Shariat and Sunnat and defending the Companions.

Hence with limited resources and few scholars, the foundation of this blessed work was laid and in the supervision of elders, a unified platform named Tahfuz-e-Sunnat Media was formed for unstipulated representation of truth. Eventually, thousands of common Muslim folk began associating with it and proved their religious commitment and zeal.

(1) WhatsApp Service :

Through Tahfuz-e-Sunnat Media, a total of five groups are active for scholars and commoners on the well-known social network WhatsApp on which only relevant content is posted and this content is shared with the general public by more than 900 representatives, the complete report of which is available at the office and to keep this service vibrant and active, short content in the form of video and audio clips and scanned images is posted daily; in this way more than 100 video clips, more than 250 images and numerous text messages and audios have gained wide reception.

(2) Monthly Traning Workshop :

To create awareness in the public, to benefit from the scholars and to timely access information on various important issues, monthly workshop is held at the office itself; in addition, programmes on various subjects are held in city and districts frequently. In this way, in the city and outside, thousands of people have benefited so far; and the numbers of madrasa students benefiting from the workshops is additional.

(3) Sermon Group :

In the context of prevailing situations, connection with public has now been confined to Friday and Eid prayers; hence considering this a blessing, a sermon group comprising more than 100 scholars has been created through which content based on prevalent circumstances is posted every Thursday so that Imams and scholars could benefit from it and create awareness in public through Friday and Eid sermons.

(4) Online Magazine :

There are hundreds of monthly periodicals and magazines that are published in our country but the void that was felt in connection with doing work on the sensitive subject of rejecting Ghair-Muqallid, after the demise of Hazrat Moulana Abu Bakr Ghazipuri (RH), required a serious and effective initiative; hence, on constant reminder and attention of the elders of the community and at the behest of Hazrat Aqdas Mohtamim Sahab (DB) (Dar-ul-Uloom, Deoband), an online magazine named “ZamZam Jadeed” was launched and it is being broadcast online every month; however when resources are available, permanent printing process shall also begin.

(5) Preparation of curriculum for summer holidays

The new generation receiving contemporary education is usually unaware of even the basics of religion due to which various groups set their curriculum and distribute free of cost at different places and our immaculate people especially youth being influenced with them not only fall prey to these false ideas but also are becoming a tool of them. In these unfortunate and pathetic circumstances, a short curriculum based on the five departments of Islam namely faith, worship, issues, culture and ethics is being developed; the youth could study it easily from their homes and appear for examination and become eligible for suitable prizes.
